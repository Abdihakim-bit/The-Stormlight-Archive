Introduction

This is a fansite solely and wholly about one of my favorite ongoing epic fantasy novels, The Stormlight Archive.

The Stormlight Archive is a continuing collection of epic fantasy novels by American author Brandon Sanderson. On 31 August 2010, the first of ten proposed books, The Way of Kings, was released. The second, Words of Radiance, was published on 4 March 2014. The third, Oathbringer, was published on 14 November 2017. The fourth novel, Rythym of War, is expected to be released on 17 November 2020.

List of books in chronological order and release dates:

Words of Radiance - 2014

Oathbringer - 2017
